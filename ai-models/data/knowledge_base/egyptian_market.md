# Egyptian Tech Job Market Guide

## Market Overview (2024-2026)

Egypt's tech sector is experiencing rapid growth, with major hubs in Cairo (Smart Village, New Cairo), Alexandria, and emerging centers in Mansoura and Assiut. International companies are increasingly setting up development centers here.

---

## In-Demand Tech Roles in Egypt

### Highest Demand (Easy to find jobs)
1. **Backend Developer** (Python/Django, Node.js, Java/Spring)

- Cloud Architects (AWS, Azure, GCP certified)
- Cybersecurity Specialists

## Salary Negotiation in Egypt

### Know Your Worth
Research on:
- LinkedIn Salary Insights
- Glassdoor Egypt
- Community salary surveys
- Ask in developer communities anonymously

### Negotiation Tips
1. **Never give your current salary first** - Say "I'm looking for X based on market rate"
2. **Have a range ready** - Know your minimum and ideal
3. **Consider total package** - Base + bonuses + insurance + remote days + learning budget
4. **Get it in writing** - Verbal offers mean nothing
5. **It's okay to walk away** - Don't accept under market rate out of desperation

### Benefits to Negotiate
- Medical insurance (for family too)
- Remote work days (2-3 days/week becoming standard)
- Learning budget (conferences, courses)
- Stock options (for startups)
- Work visa sponsorship (for international moves)

## Working Remotely from Egypt

### Legal Considerations
- Register as freelancer at Tax Authority
- Open USD bank account (CIB, QNB work well)
- Payment methods: Payoneer, PayPal (limited), Wise, bank wire
- Tax rate: ~10-22.5% (consult accountant)

### Time Zone Advantages
- Egypt (UTC+2) overlaps well with:
  - Europe (perfect overlap)
  - US East Coast (3-4 hours overlap afternoon/evening)
  - Middle East (same time zone)

### Remote Work Tips
- Invest in good internet (Vodafone or Orange fiber)
- Have backup internet (mobile hotspot)
- Set up proper home office
- Join co-working spaces for networking (GrEEK Campus, The District, Fab Lab)

## Career Growth in Egyptian Companies

### Typical Progression
Year 1-2: Junior Developer → Mid-Level
Year 3-4: Mid-Level → Senior
Year 5+: Senior → Lead/Manager or Specialist Track

### When to Switch Jobs
- No salary increase for 2+ years
- No learning opportunities
- Toxic work environment
- Better offer (20%+ increase worth considering)
- Career stagnation

### Building Your Brand
- Write technical blog posts (Arabic or English)
- Speak at local meetups
- Contribute to open source
- Mentor juniors (great for leadership track)
- Build a personal project that people use
