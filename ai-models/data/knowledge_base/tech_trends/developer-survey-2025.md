# Developer Technology Trends — Stack Overflow Survey 2025 (Attributed Summary)

## Headline language trends

According to the Stack Overflow Developer Survey 2025 (survey.stackoverflow.co/2025),
JavaScript remains the most-used programming language, used by about 66% of
respondents. Python recorded the largest year-over-year growth of the major
languages, up roughly 7 percentage points versus 2024 — driven substantially
by data and AI/ML work (sources: survey.stackoverflow.co/2025/technology;
corroborated by enstacked.com survey insights).

## How to read this as a learner in Egypt

- **JavaScript/TypeScript** dominance reflects demand across frontend and
  full-stack roles — consistent with the in-demand roles ITIDA reports for
  Egypt's offshoring sector (see the Egypt ICT Sector Outlook document in
  this knowledge base).
- **Python's growth** aligns with data science, machine-learning and
  automation roles; it is also the most common first language in Egyptian
  government training tracks such as DEPI.
- Survey caveat: respondents are self-selected, globally distributed Stack
  Overflow users — treat percentages as global directional signals, not
  Egyptian market shares (bias noted in DATASET_REGISTRY.md).
